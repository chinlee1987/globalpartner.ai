How can I experience Global Partner's service for free?

You can register an account for free here, use the G coins included in the free membership to create video synthesis for free, and experience other AI products and services. You can also purchase and upgrade the membership service, and we will provide you with more service rights and the duration of making videos.

After purchasing a membership, what is the included G coins and what is its function?

G coins is a virtual currency provided by Global Partner AI, which can be used in Global Partner AI product services. After you purchase and upgrade to a Global Partner AI member, the corresponding G coins will be included. When you create digital human video, AI text dubbing service, AI model and other services, the corresponding G coins will be deducted according to the length of the video generated by your creation or the number of times it is used. When the Global Partner AI membership you purchased expires, you can also continue to use our products and services by recharging G coins separately. At the same time, the G coins balance you have will not expire, but you cannot withdraw cash, transfer it to others, or refund it.

What happens if the purchased Global Partner AI membership plan expires?

After the purchase of the corresponding membership plan service expires, you will lose the corresponding membership service rights; but your G coins will continue to be kept in your account, and can continue to be used to use free characters for video production and synthesis or use our provided Other AI products and services. Your G coins balance will not expire, you can continue to upgrade and purchase a membership plan that suits you.

Can I upgrade my account membership service plan at any time?

Can be upgraded at any time. If you want to create more video content and long video composites, you can re-purchase the membership plan at any time. The service cycle of your membership plan will also be extended accordingly, and the K currency balance of your account will also be credited to the K currency included in the corresponding plan. You can also recharge separately to buy more G coins for more video creation.

How is the G coins value consumed?

G coins will be deducted only when the video is successfully generated. Your video is in a draft state, or you are using the trial playback function, and G coins will not be consumed. If you have generated it, the system will deduct the corresponding G coins. If you voluntarily delete the voice or video content of the creative content, we will not refund the G coins. Please note: In the case of creation failure or timeout exception, we will refund you the G coins corresponding to the deducted consumption, please feel free to use it.
